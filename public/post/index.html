Origins

Today is July 20th, 2025. 56 years ago today, humanity took its first baby steps on an incredible journey to another world. Reaching the moon was no small task. Set out following President John F. Kennedy’s challenge to the nation, NASA accomplished what no other nation to date has done. In my spare time, I participate in an Amateur Radio program called NASA On The Air. This organization participates in radio activations to commemorate milestones in American and Human spaceflight. Including all of the Apollo Missions.
